# Week 1-2 — Plan for Modeling

## Goals

- Develop a Bridge Tower Section where two towers are connected through a cross beam for one given section of the bridge (see Figure 1 — the original 1940 Tacoma Narrows tower design).

- Develop a few different platform/deck scenes, since the roadway structure varies depending on the location on the bridge (see Figure 2 — trusses visible in the middle section of the bridge):
  - One section with trusses (middle of bridge / main span)
  - One section with a minimal platform (ends / approach spans)
  - Additional scenes may be needed for details such as cables
